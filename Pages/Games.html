FAQ!

What are Comedy Sportz games?

Comedy Sportz games are intense, head to head comedy improv competitions between two teams of four. Each team plays various improv games, getting completely random suggetions from the audience in order to preform them with complete spontaneity. The audience then votes for their favorite performance through the volume of their applause.

Is it actually all thought up on the spot?

Yes! To prove that our suggestions are well and truly suggested by the audience itself, and not members of the team, all of the team members must sit in the far left wing of the audience, and not contribute any suggestions whatsoever.

How much is admission?

Admission is $5 for Students and $10 for Adults.
